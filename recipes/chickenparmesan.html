Chicken Parmesan

Learn how to bring this classic Italian dish to your dinner table!

If you have enjoyed my previous recipes, you're really going to love this one! Another classic italian dish that is easy to make and brings the restaurant to your home. Let's get started!

Ingredients

*Pro tip: Check your cabinets and fridge because this recipe is so simple you're bound to find all the needed ingredients laying around!

*Serves 4-5 people

For The Chicken

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsely
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 large chicken breast halved horizontally to make 6 filets
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic or onion powder
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

For The Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced gariic
  • 14 ounces tomato puree
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

For The Topping

  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese sliced or shredded
  • 1/3 cup fresh shredded parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped basil or parsley
