Shrimp Alfredo

Learn how to make this delicious and easy Shrimp Alfredo

If you're a pasta fanatic like I am, I know you are going to love this simple but delicious Shrimp Alfredo recipe. This dish can take you on tasteful adventure to Italy but in the comfort of your own home. A couple of ingredients, an empty stomach and the desire for a creamy pasta dish is all that is needed! Lets get started!

Ingredients

The great part about this dish, the ingredients (except for shrimp), are common items we usually keep stocked at home! Simple, Right?

*Please note the following ingredients serve 4 to 5 people

Alfredo Sauce

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 4 oz freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 lb fettucine noodles (or desired pasta)

Shrimp

  • 1.5 lbs large shrimp, thawed and peeled, tails optional
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter (I prefer butter for the added flavor)
  • fresh chopped parsley, for garnish of course (no one enjoys parsley for its taste)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package. Drain and set aside.
  2. Grate parmesan cheese and set aside, to allow it to come to room temperature.
  3. Heat a large sauce pan over medium heat. Add butter and cook until melted. Add garlic, stirring frequently for 1 minute. Add cream and whisk to combine, bring to a simmer and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring often.
  4. Turn heat to low and add parmesan cheese, whisking until smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add pasta and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Season shrimp with salt and pepper. Melt 1 tablespoon of butter or oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add shrimp and cook, stirring occasionally, unitl pink, about 2-3 minutes. Serve noodles with a scoop of shrimp on top. Garnish with parsely, if desired.
